✦ Who We Are

A Region Built on
Prayer & Holiness

MFM SW58 Araromi Akure is a regional headquarters of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, a fast-growing, Holy Spirit-filled ministry that emphasises holiness, prayer, and the manifestation of divine power.

Our regional office coordinates 8 vibrant branch churches across Akure, empowering every believer to walk in victory, deliverance and the fullness of God's blessings.
