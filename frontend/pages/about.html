✦ Our Story

Who We Are

MFM SW58 Araromi Akure is a regional headquarters of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM), founded by Dr. Daniel Kolawole Olukoya. MFM is a Holy Ghost-filled, Bible-based, prayer-oriented ministry that emphasises holiness, spiritual warfare, and God's miraculous power.

Our SW58 Araromi regional office was established to coordinate and oversee the growth and operations of MFM branches across the Akure metropolis and surrounding communities in Ondo State.

Today, the region comprises the Headquarters and 8 vibrant branch churches, each led by anointed ministers committed to winning souls, discipling believers, and demonstrating the power of God.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To bring back the Apostolic signs and wonders through fervent prayer, preaching the undiluted gospel, and demonstrating the power of the Holy Spirit in signs and wonders, thereby winning souls and discipling them for the kingdom of God.

🌟 Our Vision

To raise a mighty army of prayer warriors and soul winners that will take the gospel to every community in Akure and beyond, establishing a strong, Spirit-filled church that will impact generations for the glory of God.
